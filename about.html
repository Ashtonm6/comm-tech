Location

The Panorama Mountain Resort is located in Panorama, B.C, at the foot of the Panorama mountain. the mountain is adjacent to Mt. Goldie. The closest town to this resort is Invermere.

Eating

there are many places to go out to eat. There is the lodge where you can get a coffee, hot chocolate, or food from resturants inside the building. Right beside the lodge is the T-bar and grill, and the Alto resturant. At the top of the mountain, there is a place called the summit hut which serves similar things to the lodge at the bottom.

Activities

there are many things to do at this resort in both summer and winter the main attraction is the panorama mountain that is used for skiing and snowboarding in the winter, and biking in the summer. As well, there are many things to do at the bottom of the mountain. There is a store called toques and tracks where you can buy panorama merch and winter gear. In both seasons, you can go down to the pool and hot tub to warm up or cool down.

Lodging

If you choose to visit this resort, it is recommended that you find a place to stay. In the village, there are studio and loft style condos that you can rent for any amount of time. My family has a timeshare for a condo in the village. There are also hotels as well as privately ownes houses to rent. you can also stay in invermere close to the resort. It is recommended to find a place to stay close to the hill because most of the activities are set up in close proximity to it.

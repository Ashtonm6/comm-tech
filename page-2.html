Trails

In total, there are 134 winter trails and 21 summer trails on the panorama mountain. The runs are seperated into 4 different types: easy trails are green circles, intermediate trails are blue squares, hard trails are black diamonds, and very hard trials are double black diamonds! This is a great place to go even if you are new to skiing/snowboarding since there is a bunny hill as well as a run meant for getting used to it. Panorama also offers a service called rk heliski where they put you in a helicopter to fly you up the Purcell mountain to ski down. A similar service is the monster X snowcat where you get on a snowcat shuttle that takes you to the top of the Taynton bowl.
